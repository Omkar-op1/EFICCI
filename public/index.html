Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a transformative force in empowering farmers and stakeholders across the agri-value chain to become global exporters, aligning with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to drive inclusive economic growth, environmental sustainability, and global competitiveness, while positioning Indian products as world leaders in international markets.

Our Mission

To empower farmers and agri-value chain stakeholders with export training, market insights, and technical support, aligning with the SDGs. We facilitate global market access, advocate for export-friendly policies, and strengthen governance and business capacity. Committed to sustainability and quality, we position Indian products as globally preferred.
